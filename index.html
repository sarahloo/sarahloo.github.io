About

After several years working in a variety of corporate and digital communications roles, a premature mid-life crisis prompted me to pursue a long-held passion for graphic design.

I'm now completing a Bachelor of Communication Design at RMIT University, with a particular interest in people interact with digital and physical environments.

My approach to design is based on curiousity, consideration and clarity.
